About Us

We are a small Watery Is a Saudi based company and it has been incorporated recently in year 2021 with an objective of providing variety of technologies with specialty in Water technologies and services to our client.
Watery is a subsidiary of GeoSystems group of companies The Company and its Management wish to be a good corporate citizen by encouraging Saudi workforce wherever possible, to be a core value of the company and impart training on regular intervals and improve the skills of its work force on a continues basis.

Our Mission :

At Watery our mission is to provide smart Water and Energy Management solution by utilizing innovative technologies that known globally, to reduce the demand for water leakage and energy wastage thereby helping the country to reduce Co2 emission and protect our natural resources.

Our Vision :

We aim to lead the kingdom as Water Management Solution provider with widest innovative world class solutions to our customers by using best methods, process and tools.
